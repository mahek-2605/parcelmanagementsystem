Parcel Management

Welcome to the Parcel Management System

This system helps Banasthali Vidyapith students and staff manage parcel deliveries on campus. Track shipments, receive notifications, and coordinate pickups between Kutir and the Post Office.

Track Parcels

Search by parcel or student ID and view delivery status in real time.

Notifications

Get notified when parcels arrive and are ready for pickup.

Secure Access

Role-based access for students, Kutir supervisors, Post Office supervisors, and admins.

Support

For help contact helpdesk@banasthali.edu or visit the Kutir office on campus.
